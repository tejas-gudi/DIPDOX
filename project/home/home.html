What is diploma | dox?

Diploma | Dox is a platform for diploma students, where students
need to get registered to access standard study materials such as syllabus-copies,
Text-books, previous year Question-papers, e - learning support which includes
helpful websites, video resources etc. the most engrossing thing about this
site is student can access the notes provided by collage professors.

Students are uphold by redirecting to helpful tools such as :
1. Document format converters.
2. Template for MS Word and MS Power Point, for beautiful presentations.
3. Collage project-works.
4. E-learning resources.

Diploma | Dox allows collage professors to upload their notes, which is considered
very helpful for students.
